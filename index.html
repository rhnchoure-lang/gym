Common Questions

Frequently
Asked

What are your operating hours?
+

We are open Monday–Friday from 5 AM to 11 PM, Saturday from 6 AM to 10 PM, and Sunday from 7 AM to 8 PM. Our open gym hours are available to Champion plan members 24/7.

Is there a free trial available?
+

Yes! We offer a complimentary 7-day trial for all new members. No commitment required — experience our full facility, classes, and community before joining.

Do I need prior experience?
+

Not at all. Our programs cater to all levels — from complete beginners to seasoned athletes. Every member gets a personalized onboarding session with a certified trainer.

What's included in Yoga & Zumba batches?
+

Our specialized batches include Hatha Yoga, Vinyasa Flow, Power Yoga, Meditation, Zumba Fitness, Zumba Toning, Zumba Kids, and Dance Cardio. Elite and Champion plan members get unlimited access.

Can I freeze or transfer my membership?
+

Yes, you can freeze your membership for up to 30 days per year. Transfers to another person are allowed with Elite and Champion plans for a minimal admin fee.

Do you offer nutrition coaching?
+

Absolutely. Our in-house nutritionist creates personalized meal plans based on your fitness goals, dietary preferences, and body metrics. Available with Elite and Champion plans.

Is personal training available?
+

Yes. Our Elite plan includes 4 PT sessions/month, and Champion plan offers unlimited personal training. One-on-one sessions can also be purchased separately.

Do you have batches for kids?
+

Yes! We run fun, safe fitness batches for children aged 6–14 that include functional movements, flexibility, coordination, and teamwork activities supervised by trained coaches.
